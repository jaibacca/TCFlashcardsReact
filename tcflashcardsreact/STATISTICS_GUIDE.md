# Statistics Feature Guide

## Understanding Your Statistics

### 📊 Overview Cards

The top row shows your key metrics at a glance:

1. **Overall Accuracy** (Pink/Red gradient)
   - Your success rate across all drills
   - Calculated from total correct / total attempts
   - Goal: Aim for 80%+ accuracy

2. **Total Attempts** (Purple gradient)
   - How many questions you've answered overall
   - Increases each time you submit an answer

3. **Mastered Cards** (Purple gradient)
   - Cards you know well (≥3 correct attempts with ≥80% accuracy)
   - These cards need less frequent review

4. **Learning Cards** (Purple gradient)
   - Cards you've attempted but haven't mastered yet
   - Focus your study time here

### 🔥 Study Streak

- **Current Streak**: Consecutive days you've studied
- **Longest Streak**: Your personal best
- Studying on consecutive days builds your streak
- Missing a day resets the current streak to 0

### 📈 Drill Performance

Individual statistics for each drill type:

1. **Hanzi → Pinyin + English** (Green accent)
2. **Pinyin → English** (Blue accent)
3. **Pinyin → Hanzi** (Purple accent)
4. **English → Hanzi** (Orange accent)

For each drill, you'll see:
- **Attempts**: How many times you've practiced this drill
- **Correct**: How many you got right
- **Accuracy**: Success rate with color coding:
  - 🟢 Green (≥80%): Excellent!
  - 🟡 Orange (60-79%): Good, keep practicing
  - 🔴 Red (<60%): Needs more work

### 📊 Card Mastery Progress

A visual bar showing your overall progress:
- 🟢 **Green**: Mastered cards (you know these well!)
- 🔵 **Blue**: Learning cards (keep practicing)
- ⚪ **Gray**: New cards (haven't studied yet)

## How Statistics Are Updated

### During Practice:
1. Answer a question in any drill
2. Click "Check Answer"
3. Statistics are immediately updated:
   - Drill-specific attempts and accuracy
   - Card-specific history (for spaced repetition)
   - Overall accuracy
   - Study streak (if it's a new day)

### Card Mastery Criteria:
A card is considered "mastered" when:
- You've answered it correctly at least 3 times
- Your accuracy for that card is ≥80%

### Streak Rules:
- Study today → Streak continues if you studied yesterday, or starts at 1
- Miss a day → Current streak resets to 1 when you study again
- Longest streak → Always saves your best performance

## Using Statistics to Improve

### 1. Identify Weak Areas
Look at your drill performance cards:
- Red accuracy? Focus on that drill type
- Low attempts? Maybe practice it more
- High accuracy? You're doing great!

### 2. Track Card Progress
- Watch the mastery progress bar grow
- Goal: Move cards from "New" → "Learning" → "Mastered"
- Revisit learning cards frequently

### 3. Build Consistency
- Try to study daily to maintain your streak
- Even 10-15 minutes counts!
- Streaks build habits

### 4. Balance Your Practice
- Don't just practice the easy drills
- Challenge yourself with writing drills (3 & 4)
- Aim for balanced accuracy across all drill types

## Managing Your Statistics

### Viewing Stats:
- Statistics appear on the main page after loading data
- Updated in real-time as you practice
- Scroll down to see all metrics

### Clearing Stats:
1. Click the red "Clear Stats" button
2. Confirm in the dialog
3. All statistics reset to zero
4. ⚠️ This cannot be undone!

### When to Clear Stats:
- Starting a new learning phase
- Switching to a different vocabulary set
- Want a fresh start

### Data Persistence:
- Statistics are saved in your browser
- They persist even after closing the app
- Clearing browser data will also clear stats
- Each browser/device has separate statistics

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Practice Regularly**: Daily practice builds streaks and improves retention
2. **Mix It Up**: Rotate through all 4 drill types for comprehensive learning
3. **Review Weak Areas**: Focus extra time on drills with lower accuracy
4. **Celebrate Milestones**: 
   - First 10 mastered cards
   - 7-day streak
   - 80%+ overall accuracy
   - 100 total attempts

## Privacy Note

All statistics are stored locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server. Your progress is private and stays on your device.
